Hey! I'm Dan W Anderson

I'm a software engineer/data scientist living in Chicago IL. I like to think about things, and then overthink them, whether it's programming, philosophy, science fiction, homebrewing, or long distance running.

Why this blog?

If you've ever found yourself toiling through some unnecessarily difficult programming task, or wondering about the ethical implications of sentient AI, this site will hopefully help.

I like learning, and sharing what I've learned with others, and hearing their feedback, and continuing the cycle. Some of the most interesting and important insights come about accidentally and randomly - nothing is more fascinating to me than when a seemingly unrelated topic suddenly becomes perfectly intertwined.

Fun Facts:

  • I ♥ GitHub
  • I'm not sure which Douglas is my favorite: Hofstadter or Adams
  • I homebrew beer, and my favorite so far has been a Cold Brew Coffee Porter
  • I'd like to run a marathon sometime in 2017
